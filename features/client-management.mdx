## Overview

Client Management allows you to maintain a comprehensive database of your customers, whether they are individual persons or companies. This information is essential for creating purchase and sale contracts and maintaining customer relationships.

## Client Types

SGIVU supports two types of clients:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Individual Persons" icon="user">
    Private individuals purchasing or selling vehicles. Includes personal information and contact details.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Companies" icon="building">
    Business entities engaged in vehicle transactions. Includes corporate information and business contact details.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Managing Individual Clients

### Adding a New Person

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Client Management">
    Access the Client Management section from your dashboard. You need the `person:create` permission to add new individual clients.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Personal Information">
    Fill in the client's details:
    - **Full Name**: First and last name
    - **Email Address**: Primary contact email
    - **Phone Number**: Contact phone number
    - **Date of Birth**: For age verification and record-keeping
    - **Identification**: ID number or driver's license (if required)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Address Information">
    Enter the client's address:
    - Street address
    - City
    - State/Province
    - Postal code
    - Country
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Notes (Optional)">
    Include any additional information about the client's preferences, requirements, or transaction history.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Client">
    Review all information and save the new client record. The client is now available for use in contracts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Updating Person Information

<Card title="Edit Client Details" icon="pen-to-square">
  Update client information at any time by searching for the person and selecting edit. Changes are reflected immediately across all related records.
</Card>

## Managing Company Clients

### Adding a New Company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Company Management">
    From Client Management, select the Companies tab. You need the `company:create` permission to add new corporate clients.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Company Information">
    Fill in the corporate details:
    - **Company Name**: Legal business name
    - **Tax ID**: Business tax identification number
    - **Email Address**: Primary business contact email
    - **Phone Number**: Main business phone line
    - **Industry**: Type of business (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Business Address">
    Enter the company's registered or primary business address with complete details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Contact Person (Optional)">
    If applicable, include the name and contact information for the primary contact person at the company.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Company">
    Review all information and save the company record. The company is now available for business transactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Updating Company Information

<Card title="Edit Company Details" icon="building">
  Company information can be updated at any time. This is particularly useful when business details change or new contact persons are assigned.
</Card>

## Integration with Contracts

Client records are directly linked to purchase and sale contracts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Client Selection">
    When creating a new contract, search and select the client from your database.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Population">
    Client information automatically populates contract fields, reducing data entry and errors.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Transaction History">
    View all contracts associated with a client from their profile page, creating a complete transaction history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cannot create new clients">
    Verify you have the `person:create` or `company:create` permission. Ensure all required fields are filled correctly.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Duplicate client warning">
    The system may warn you if similar client information already exists. Verify this isn't a duplicate before proceeding.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Search returns no results">
    Check your search terms and filters. Try broader search criteria or verify the client exists in the database.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot update client information">
    Ensure you have the `person:update` or `company:update` permission. Check that you're not violating any data validation rules.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Address validation fails">
    Verify all address fields are filled correctly. Some fields may have specific format requirements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
